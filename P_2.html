Frequently Asked Questions

# How do I Make a regular Table Booking?

It's a piece of cake - once you've simply select your Date and Time required, and then so long as we have an email address and phone number your booking is instantly confirmed the minute you hit "Book"

# Is it open every day of the week?

We close on Monday evening for our weekly rest.

We are open every day for the rest of the year.

# Are there kid's menus?

We do not have a set kids’ menu but we do offer alternatives for children on request.

# Does the restaurant have a telephone number to reserve a table?

Yes, it is 01222 01222

# What's the capacity on the restaurant?

120 people

# At what time can we have lunch and dinner in the restaurant?

Lunch is serverd from 13:00-15:30

Dinner is serverd from 20:00-22:30

# Can i choose between the dining room and the terrace? Can i book a table on the terrace?

You can book a table on the terrace. However, if the weather is bad, these reservations are not guaranteed because we cannot be sure that the reservation can be moved to the dining room (it may be full).

If the weather is bad, reservations on the terrace are cancelled as there are no awnings or glass enclosures to allow us to serve food.

In any event, we always try to move all reservations inside the restaurant.

# At what time can we have breakfast in the restaurant?

Currently we dont serve breakfast, we are thinking to get started with it in the future.
